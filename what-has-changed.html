What Has Changed in the 2025 Finance Bill?

A breakdown of the key differences between the previous Finance Bill and the new 2025 version.

1. New Taxes Introduced

The 2025 bill introduces new taxes on digital content, mobile money transfers, and certain food items.

2. VAT Adjustments

Value Added Tax (VAT) on essential items like bread has been reinstated at 16% â€” previously zero-rated.

3. Income Tax Bands Revised

New income brackets and higher tax rates for higher earners have been introduced.

4. Penalties and Compliance

Stricter penalties and shorter compliance windows have been proposed to increase efficiency.

5. Youth and SME Incentives

Tax holidays and reductions for registered youth-led enterprises and SMEs for up to 3 years.
