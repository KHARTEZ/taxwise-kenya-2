What Has Changed in the Finance Bill 2025?

1. VAT on Bread Returns

For the first time since 2013, bread will be taxed at 16% VAT again, impacting everyday costs.

2. Annual Motor Vehicle Circulation Tax

This is a completely new tax based on engine capacity, payable annually by all car owners.

3. Digital Content Monetization

Taxation for creators is now formalized. Anyone earning online — from YouTube, TikTok, etc. — must pay 15% tax.

4. Adjustments in PAYE Brackets

New tax brackets introduced for middle-income earners — some relief for lower earners, but higher bands taxed more.
