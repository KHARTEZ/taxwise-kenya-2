Finance Bill 2025 – Explained Simply

1. What is the Finance Bill?

The Finance Bill 2025 is a proposed set of laws that determine how the government will collect and spend money in the upcoming year.

2. Why it matters

The bill introduces new tax policies, affects prices of goods/services, and impacts every Kenyan's day-to-day life.

3. Key Highlights

- VAT increase on some essential goods
- New digital tax policies
- Proposed changes to PAYE and NHIF contributions
