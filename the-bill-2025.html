Finance Bill 2025 - Clause-by-Clause Breakdown

Clause 5 – Income Tax on Digital Content Creators

New taxes to be imposed on YouTubers, TikTokers, and other digital income earners at 15% flat rate.

Clause 12 – Motor Vehicle Circulation Tax

Introduction of annual tax on car owners between KES 5,000 and KES 100,000 based on engine size.

Clause 20 – VAT on Bread

Reintroduction of 16% VAT on bread, expected to increase prices across all retail chains.
