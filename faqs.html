Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is the Finance Bill 2025?

The Finance Bill 2025 is a proposed law that outlines changes to taxation and revenue collection in Kenya.

2. Why is it controversial?

Many people believe it introduces heavy taxes that may burden common citizens and businesses.

3. What does TAXWISE-KE do?

We simplify the clauses, explain the effects of proposed changes, and help Kenyans understand how they are affected.
